Western Australia Shipwreck Risk Assessment

The coast of Western Australia is home to over 1,400 shipwrecks, which are at threat of major deterioration due to climate change. Factors such as sea level rise, high temperatures, salinity, and pH have lead to an increased risk of shipwreck degredation.

To preserve Australia's coastal and underwater heritage, this map aims to raise awareness of this issue. Go here for more information.
